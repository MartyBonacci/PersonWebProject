About Me

Hello! My name is Mark Waid Jr, I am a Deep Dive Coding Bootcamp alumni. I have always loved tech, and it has been a dream of mine to work in the tech industry, specifically the software development side of things. I started my technology journey when I was still in high school, I took a basic computer programming class, and from there became hooked. I have other interests besides just coding and tech. I also love video games; yes I know it's not a big difference, but video games are a tech beast all their own. For relaxation I love camping, hiking, spending my time out doors, and relaxing lazily at home. Thanks for taking the time to read about me, I hope you enjoy looking at the rest of my site.
